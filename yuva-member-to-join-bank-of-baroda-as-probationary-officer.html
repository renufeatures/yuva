Yuva member to join Bank Of Baroda as Probationary Officer

By yuvajamshedpur
May 17, 2012
0
Din Dayal Dubey, an active Yuva Jamshedpur member, has been selected as Probationary Officer by Bank of Baroda.

Jamshedpur: It is yet another success story for Yuva Jamshedpur members. One more of its members is all set to join Bank Of Baroda as probationary officer.

And it is a moment of extreme pride for all the Yuva Jamshedpur family. Din Dayal Dubey, who has been actively associated with Yuva Jamshedpur, has been successful in the highly competitive recruitment test and interview for probationary officers' post in Bank of Baroda.
Din Dayal Dubey has been an enthusiastic member of Yuva Jamshedpur and he is known as a hard-working, dedicated and intelligent member. He is also known as a member who makes optimum use of Yuva Library.

He could be seen browsing through the magazines and books even at odd hours. Thanks to his single-mindedness, he has been successful in his endeavour. Now he has become a role model for other aspiring students preparing for competitive examinations.

Yuva has had a rich history of helping students with modest family backgrounds achieve top success in their career and academic life. A large number of its members have joined various government and non-government jobs and in banks as POs and clerks.
In a special message to Yuva Members, Din Dayal Dubey expressed his affection for other members in these words: "I am very happy to inform you that I've been selected in Bank of baroda as P.O. and I'm really grateful to Arvind Bhaiya, founder of YUVA Jamshedpur, YUVA Library and all the members of the YUVA. It would have been impossible for me to achieve this success, had it not been for the great support from YUVA Library that I received at different stages. I also want to tell each member of YUVA that there is no short cut to success and there is nothing that can stop you from achieving success if you stick to your aim devotedly. Be patient and give your best! Success will surely be at your feet. I thank Yuva and Arvind Bhaiya and every member of Yuva for all the love and affection I enjoyed during my stint as a Yuva member."

We too wish Din Dayal Dubey all the best. We know he would be a very nice and efficient bank officer and would do everything to help the common man. We know you will keep the torch of honesty and social responsibility alight!
