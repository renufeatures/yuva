Yuva Jamshedpur organizes Bal Mela at Chakdi School, gives children joyous moments

By yuvajamshedpur
January 31, 2013
0
Children playing in Bal Mela

YUVA organized a Bal mela at Chakdi middle School under Shiksha se parivrtan project and gave scores of children happy and joyous moments. The children enjoyed themselves. Beaming faces of happy children made the event a huge success.

SHGs and SMC members worked hard to make the Bal Mela a grad success.More than 200 children from 15 schools of the nearby areas participated in this Bal Mela.

They also arranged for food for the participating children. Shiksa Se Parivatan project is being implemented by YUVA for ensuring improvement in quality of education in government schools spread across 2o villages in Potka. The project is being implemented with the active help of EDUKANS, SPAR and Development Focus.

Children in sports competition in Yuva Jamshedpur Bal Mela

The Bal Mela was inaugurated by Shiv Jan Sardar, Headmaster, Chakdi Middle School.

In Bal mela many competitions were organized. These included Maths Race,  General Knowledge test, music, elocution, handicraft, drawing, long jump, high jump, 100 meter race and 150 meter race.

The winners of different competitions were awarded by the village head Mr Puroshotam Sardar, the former head master Mr. Jai Hari Singh Munda, the headmaster Mr. Shiv Jan Sardar and the members of five SHGs.

Time for lunch in Bal Mela organized by Yuva Jamshedpur

The competition and prize distribution ceremony were conducted by the cordinater Abhishek Singh Sardar and Arup Mandal.
