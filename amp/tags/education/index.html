Yuva observes Foundation Day (Pix: Uttam Acharjee)

Jamshedpur: The members of social organization Youth Unity For Voluntary Action (Yuva), Jamshedpur today celebrated its Foundation day at Yuva Library at Makdampur, Parsudih. Five Savar children to study in KPT Residential School in Patamda

Jamshedpur: Five Savar children, who have been educated and mainstreamed at Kislay- a school for Savar children run by Yuva and Asha For Education, will now stay in a residential school at Dhusra, Patamda.

All these children have been admitted into Kerala Public Trust Residential School run at Dhusra, Patamda. The children who have been selected for education in the residential school include Ajay Sabar and Sukra Sabar from Dehngam village and Ganga Sabar, Susru Sabar and Soma Sabar from Tangrain village.

All these five children are very keen on joining the residential school and living and studying with other children there.

Bal Mela brings joy to children

Jamshedpur: Yuva, Jamshedpur organized a Bal Mela (Child Conference) on March 17 at Chakdi Middle School. Children from 24 different schools participated in this conference. This programme was organized with the help of Educance, Development Focus and SPAR as a part of Shiksha Se Parivartan campaign.

Bal Sansad (Children's Parliament), plays, quiz and handicraft competitions were organized during the conference.

The first prize in Bal Sansad went to Chhota Hadiayan Middle School, while the second and third prizes were won by Nachosai Upgraded Middle School and Kisandih Primary School respectively.
