Rural Development Secy visits Yuva's dairy

By yuvajamshedpur
August 13, 2010

Jamshedpur (Turamdih): Mr D K Srivastava, secretary,Rural Development, Govt of Jharkhand today visted YUVA supported Dairy farm - 'Disha Doodh Utpadan Kendra' at  Aaharguttu, Turamdih, Jamshedpur.

This Dairy Farm is being run efficiently by the women's self-help groups Marshal Mahila samity and Taras Mahila Samity. Both SHGs have been formed at the initiative of YUVA and are working as a model examples in the area of women empowerment.

The Secretary, rural development, Jharkhand was also accompanied by the the Directer of DRDA, East singhbhum Mrs Uma Shashi Chatterji and the  BDO of Jamshedpur, Mr Sunil Kumar.  also visited the Dairy.

Mr srivastava praised the initiatives taken by Yuva and its members and sought to see the vision plan for development of the organization.

Yuva secretary Mrs Barnali Chakraborty welcomed the guests. Maya Hansda and Devla Tudu, members of the SHGs briefed the guests about the functioning of the Dairy as well as its management.
