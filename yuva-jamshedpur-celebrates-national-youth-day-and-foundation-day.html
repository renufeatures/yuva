Yuva Jamshedpur celebrates National Youth Day and Foundation Day

By yuvajamshedpur
January 13, 2017
0
Yuva Founder Arbind Tiwary inaugurating National Youth Day cum Foundation Day programme at Makdampur, Parsudih, Jamshedpur. (Pix: Uttam Acharjee)

Jamshedpur: January 12th is the foundation day of Yuva Jamshedpur. It is also the birth anniversary of Swami Vivekanand.

Like every year, this year too Youth Unity For Voluntary Action (Yuva Jamshedpur) organised its foundation day cum Youth Day today at Makdampur, Parsudih.

The founder member of Yuva, Jamshedpur, Mr Arvind Tiwary today inaugurated the foundation day programme by lighting a lampion in front of a photo of Swami Vivekanand.

Addressing the programme, he said that starting this year Yuva would be starting a novel Yuva Samvaad programme, which would provide an opportunity to the youths of East Singhbhum an opportunity to interact wth doyens and leaders of different fields and get inspired by their success.

Yuva celebrated National Youth Day cum Foundation Day programme at Makdampur, Parsudih, Jamshedpur. (Pix: Uttam Acharjee)

He said that Yuva Samvaad programme would be based on the theme ‘Listen, think and learn’ or ‘Suno, Sochi, samjho’.

A quiz was also organised on the occasion. The quiz was conducted by Md. Anis.

The team of Vaibhav, Atul and Deepu won the first place in the quiz, while the team comprising Sumit, Amit, Avinash and Ashu Srivastava secured the second place in the quiz.

Yuva Founder Arbind Tiwary inaugurating National Youth Day cum Foundation Day programme at Makdampur, Parsudih, Jamshedpur. (Pix: Uttam Acharjee)

Those who participated in the programme included Usha, Sabina Deogam, Jyoti Pingua, Shalini Srivastava, Anjana, Sonu, Deepu, Ranvir and Kanchan.
