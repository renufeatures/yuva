Two jobs started in Rohinibeda village in Sohda panchayat

By yuvajamshedpur
May 4, 2016
Jamshedpur: Two jobs were started and 60 labourers got their registration done during the “Kaam Mango, Kaam Kholo” campaign in Rohinibeda village of Sohda Panchayat under Potka block on Tueday. The campaign was jointly inaugurated by Rasiyan Kisku, Mukhiya or Sohda Panchayat, panchayat committee member Reeta Sardar and secretary of Yuva Barnali Chakraborty.
Those who were present on the occasion included Rojgar Sevak Suphal Hansda, Gram Pradhan Girda Sardar, ward member Ahilal Sardar, Mate Secretary Tudu, CFT team leader Pramod Kumar, technical expert Shankar Kaibort, CRP Surat Gope, Jaleshwari Mardi, Jyoti Pingua, Jairam Sardar, Dulal Mandal, Harish Mahato, Arun Mahato, Uttam Acharjee, Manik Sardar, Karan Hansda, Vikram Soren, Chogo Manji etc.

At first, construction of 50 x 50 dobha was started. And then renovation work of a pond of 50 x 50 was also started.

It may be mentioned that Yuva has been working as a facilitator for carrying out NRLM MGRNEGA CFT convergence project work. Yuva has been guiding workers in starting work in villages and in seeking work.
CFT team leader Pramod Kumar said that this month the target is to ensure that at least one work continues in each village. For this, Yuva has been creating awareness in the villages.
