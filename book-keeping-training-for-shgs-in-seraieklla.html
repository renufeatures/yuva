Book-keeping training for SHGs in Seraieklla

By yuvajamshedpur
February 6, 2018
Book Keeping training for SHGs in Seraikella.

With the support of the Jharkhand Tribal Development Society (JTDS) under the Jharkhand Tribal Empowerment and Livelihood Project FNGO - YUVA has organized five day residential SHG book-keeping training at Seraikella Block for the Self Help Groups – women associated with the JTELP project through SHG module of development.

The objective of this training is to facilitate them in economic sustainability in their credit and debit method of daily savings.

The training has been schedule from date 05-02-2018 to 09-02-2018 in the venue of Tribal Agriculture Development Center; Balrampur at Pandra Panchayat.

In this training program participated participants are from five project village on-going in the following Panchayats – Itakudar; Murkum; Pandra; Pathanmara and Chotadawna.

On the first day; 05.02.2018; Session was started with the registration of 80 participants from 10:00 AM morning.

Followed by a prayer of SHG and session continued according to the schedule time frame i.e. Bathiki Book of SHG; Discussion of Cash book and Leasure book and maintain the same.

Book Keeping training for SHGs in Seraikella.

After Lunch; Flower of the session was taken on a topic regarding Individual Cash books maintain. The session of the first day ended with happy faces and learning interaction.
