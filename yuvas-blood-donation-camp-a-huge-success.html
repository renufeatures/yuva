Yuva's blood donation camp a huge success

By yuvajamshedpur
September 22, 2012
0
Yuva members donating blood

Jamshedpur: Yuva organized a blood donation camp this year too. The blood donation camp was organized in collaboration with Sri Shyama Puja Committee, Railway Traffic Colony, Tatanagar. The blood donation camp coincided with the arrival of Red Ribbon Express at Tatanagar railway station. In all, 85 persons donated blood in the camp. Arvind Singh, MLA, Ichagarh was the chief guest of the blood donation camp. Rambachan, director, Indra ITI was the guest of honour.
Among the distinguished persons who graced the blood donation camp were Dr. Maheshwar Prasad, S K Choudhary of Jamshedpur Blood Bank. S K Singh and S Raju from Blood Donors' Association also were present in the camp.

The blood donation was organized with the active help from Voluntary Blood Donors' Association, Sudha Dairy and Indra ITI.

The blood donation camp started with a welcome addressed by Barnali Chakraborty, secretary, Yuva.

Yuva members donating blood

Those who worked hard to make the blood donation camp a huge success included Arbind Tiwary, founder, Yuva, Kali Rao, Manoj Lal, Shantu Bhattacharya, Ranjit Kumar Chouhan, Nanda, Mahendra, Prashanto, Abhijit, Sanjay Kumar, Achhelal, S B Raju, Rajesh Yadav, Virendra Rai, Subhash Pal, Nepal, Pramod, Chandmani Sawainya, Jagat, Kishore Yadav, Shankaracharya, Salomi Barla, Rajkumar Pandey and Sumit Kumar.

Yuva members donating blood

Those who helped in the conduct of the blood donation camp included Dr. L B Singh of Jamshedpur Blood Bank, Sister S M Tudu, M D Raza, Anil Kumar and S K Singh, A K Mazumdar, J P Mitra, Krishna Mazumdar and Dinanath Singh from VBDA.
