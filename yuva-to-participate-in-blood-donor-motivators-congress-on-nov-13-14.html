Yuva to participate in Blood Donor Motivators Congress on Nov 13-14

By yuvajamshedpur
June 26, 2010
YUVA will participate in Global Congress of YouthÂ  Blood donor Motivators to be held in Kolkata on November 13-14, 2010. Arbind Tiwary, founder member, Yuva, will represent the organization in this Global Congress of Youth Blood Donor Motivators Congress.

Mr. Tiwary will also present a paper on "Voluntary Blood Donation Movement in Jamshedpur" in the Congress.

Yuva has been at the forefront of blood donation movement in the steel city of Jamshedpur and organizes blood donation camps regularly.

Thanks to voluntary social organizations, Jamshedpur is one of the leading cities of India, where blood donation has developed into a well-directed social movement. Each month, scores of blood donation camps are organized in the city by social organizations, colleges, trade unions and private and public sector enterprises.

The city also boasts of a well-run Blood Bank and there is virtually no dearth of blood in the city, thanks to its magnanimous blood donors who donate blood regularly.
