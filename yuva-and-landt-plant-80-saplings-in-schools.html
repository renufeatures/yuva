Yuva and L&T plant 80 saplings in schools

By yuvajamshedpur
June 23, 2015
Yuva and L&T planted trees in 2 schools.

Jamshedpur: Social organization Youth Unity for Voluntary Action (Yuva) in collaboration with Larsen & Toubro company today organized a plantation campaign in Janata Madhya Vidyalaya, Chhota Govindpur and Gadra Government Middle School.
Mr. Nitin Agrawal, branch manager, L & T was the chief guest of the plantation programme and he launched it by planting saplings.

L&T Branch Manager Mr. Nitin Agrawal planting saplings at Janata Middle School, Chhota Govindpur.

Those who were present included Mr Rana Pratap Singh, head master, Janata Middle School, Mrs. Bani Ekka, head mistress, Gadra Middle School and Arbind Tiwary, Assistant Teacher, Sarojini Hansda, Sanjay Lata, Sangeeta Richards, Minz Iva Dadel, Samapti Choudhary, Nutan Anita Khalko, Mahanty Anjali Toppo, Abha Rani, Savitri Singh and Anil Prasad. Rajesh Sinha, Girish Mishra, Frank Michael, P S Bhattacharya and Barun Khatri too planted saplings on the occasion. In all 80 saplings were planted in the two schools. Mr. Nitin Agrawal, branch manager, L & T and other guests also awarded all the winners different competitions on the occasions. The programme was compered by Arbind Tiwary.
