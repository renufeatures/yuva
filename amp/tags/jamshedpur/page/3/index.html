Ardhendu Chatterjee inspects Yuva-supported home gardens, shares valuable tips on home gardening

Jamshedpur: Ardhendu Chatterjee, a well-known social worker of Kolkata visted Podahatu village of Potka. Four Sabar children to join Bharat Sevashram Sangh's residential school

Bal Mela brings joy to children

Jamshedpur: Yuva, Jamshedpur organized a Bal Mela (Child Conference) on March 17 at Chakdi Middle School. Children from 24 different schools participated in this conference. This programme was organized with the help of Educance, Development Focus and SPAR as a part of Shiksha Se Parivartan campaign.

Bal Sansad (Children's Parliament), plays, quiz and handicraft competitions were organized during the conference.

The first prize in Bal Sansad went to Chhota Hadiayan Middle School, while the second and third prizes were won by Nachosai Upgraded Middle School and Kisandih Primary School respectively.

Women learn puffed-rice making in Chakri and Radur villages in Potka

Jamshedpur: A puffed-rice making training session was organized on February 12, 2012 at Chakri and Radur villages of Potka block in East Singhbhum by Yuva to enable women to start village level puffed-rice enterprises.

Yuva has always laid a special stress on promoting self-employment and enterprise among rural women. The organization believes that real empowerment of rural women would come only if they learn to be self-dependent economically. And Yuva also believes that there is no lack of ability among the rural women.
