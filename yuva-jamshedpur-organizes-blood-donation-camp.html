Yuva Jamshedpur organizes blood donation camp

By yuvajamshedpur
April 19, 2015
Blood donation camp at Yuva, Jamshedpur. (Pix: Uttam Acharjee)

Jamshedpur: Youth Unity For Voluntary Action (YUVA), Jamshedpur organized a blood donation camp at Yuva Library at Makdampur, Parsudih. In all, 40 persons donated blood in the camp.
Those who donated blood in the blood donation camp included the founder member Arbind Tiwary, Arun Kumar, Mamata Kumari, Yuva president Barun Kumar, treasurer Narendra Kumar, Rajesh Kumar, Arpita Srivastava, Navin Kumar, Vijay, in-charge, Library, Avinash and Blood Donation Camp in-charge Suraj Kumar.
