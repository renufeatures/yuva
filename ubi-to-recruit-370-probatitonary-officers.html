UBI to recruit 370 Probatitonary Officers

By yuvajamshedpur
August 21, 2010
0

The United Bank of India, a leading nationalized public sector bank, has invited online application from Indian citizens for 370 posts of Probationary Officers in JMG Scale-1.

This is a big opportunity for the unemployed youths, looking for a good job in Banks.

Candidates are requested to apply Online between 10.08.2010 and 08.09.2010 only through Bank’s website www.unitedbankofindia.com.  No other means/mode of application/Printout will be accepted. For downloading application and learning other details go to UBI website.

Yuva members desirous of making the most of this opportunity may contact the librarian for further details. If any special help is needed kindly seek guidance from the librarian.

This would be a tough competition, but there is nothing to be disheartened. In the past many Yuva members have cracked such tests and their experience can come handy for young members.
