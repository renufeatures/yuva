Donate

By Renufeatures
August 25, 2018
0

Yuva, Jamshedpur works on several projects and interventions, starting from Savar Children's Education to SHG formation and providing health facilities to remote communities.

We welcome any help from generous people and we ensure that your donation is used for the cause you specifically are interested in.

If you are interested in making any donation to us, please drop in a word. We will get in touch with you and give you all the details for donating to any of the projects, interventions being run by Yuva Jamshedpur.

We will also keep you posted about the use of your donation and would keep you involved in a transparent manner, to the extent it is possible.

Kindly, drop in a word with your phone no. and the cause you are interested in. We will get in touch with you with all requisite details.
