Yuva Jamshedpur organizes Quiz and Slogan competitions on violence against women

By yuvajamshedpur
December 11, 2014
Quiz and Slogan competition in LBSM College, Karandih, Jamshedpur. (Pix: Uttam Acharjee).

Jamshedpur: Social organization Youth Unity for Voluntary Action (Yuva) on December 10, 2014 organized a quiz and slogan competition in LBSM College on the subject of violence against women.

Yuva in association with Oxfam India has launched a joint campaign against violence against women and this campaign, which was launched on November 25, continued till December 10, 2014.
The quiz was conducted by Barnali Chakraborty, secretary, Yuva. On the other hand, the slogan competition was conducted by Chandmani Sawaiyan and Usha Deogam.

The slogan competition was organized in two groups. In Group A, Laxmi Kalindi won the first prize, Vivek Choudhary the second prize and Sunita Hembrom and Padmalochan Sardar jointly won the third prize. In Group B, Guruwari Soren and Ankit Surin won the first and second prizes.

On this occasion hundreds of students wrote postcards addressed to the Governor. Â They urged the government to expedite appointment of Protection Officer and Service Provider as provided for in Domestic Violence Act 2005.

The principal of the college, Mr. Ashok Verma and Mr. A K Jha, lecturer provided all help and co-operation to make the programme a huge success.
