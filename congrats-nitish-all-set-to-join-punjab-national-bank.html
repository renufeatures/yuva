Congrats! Nitish all set to join Punjab National Bank

By yuvajamshedpur
October 3, 2011
0

Jamshedpur: Nitish Kumar Singh, an active member of Yuva and a studious young boy, is all set to join Punjab National Bank. He has been selected for the post of clerk after clearing a tough written examination and a grueling interview.

In a message to Yuvajamshedpur.com webmaster, Nitish wrote: "I, Nitish Kumar Singh, am extremly happy to inform you that thanks to all the help and assistance from Yuva I have been recently selected for the post of clerk in Punjab National Bank. I thank and wish all the best to all the friends in Yuva and especially Arvind Bhaiyya, Barnali Didi."

Nitish has made invaluable contribution to Yuva through his proactive and spirited efforts. Nitish has always been keen to study hard and do something in life.  But, he knew, to do something in life, he first has to stand on his own feet. He needed a good job. He worked hard and kept studying and practicing  even during late hours in Yuva library with other friends.

His efforts have borne fruit. And finally Nitish is all set to join Punjab National Bank, one of the premiere public sector banks of India. His hard work and sincerity will serve as a source of inspiration for other members of Yuva.

It is yet another proud moment for Yuva and its members as another young boy from our midst leaves us to join the mainstream of society. We know the fire to do something for the society, humanity and for the underprivileged, will always remain alive in his heart and mind.

All of us wish Nitish all the best for his career and future!
