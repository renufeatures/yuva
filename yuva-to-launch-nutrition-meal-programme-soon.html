Yuva to launch Nutrition Meal Programme soon

By yuvajamshedpur
June 26, 2010
0

YUVA will launch a nutrition mealÂ  programme in different slums of Jamshedpur soon with the help of Manav Charities, Bangalore and NGO Fund-raisers.

The project is to provide nutritious meal such as breakfast, lunch and dinner among poor children, who live in different slum areas of Jamshedpur.

For this project an arrangement would be made for setting up a centralized kitchen and the food will be transported in steel containers. Food will be distributed in slums everyday, including on Sundays and public holidays.

Yuva members are excited about this ambitious Meal Programme as they feel providing fresh meal to the slum children and taking care of hygiene would be a challenging task for them. This is the first time they would be taking up such a project where meal would be distributed directly among children.

The members feel with complete dedication they would be able to carry out the task successfully. And they would be only happy to see slum children get nutritious meal regularly.
