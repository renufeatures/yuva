YUVA trains women mates of Hesalbill panchayat

By yuvajamshedpur
June 4, 2016
Jamshedpur, 2 June: A training programme was organized today for the women mates employed under the MGNREGS in Hesalbill panchayat of Potka under the aegis of Youth Unity for Voluntary Action (YUVA).

Altogether 20 women mates participated in the training programme. The trainers included YUVA secretary Barnali Chakraborty, CFT team leader Pramod Kumar, Junior Engineer (JE) of Potka block James Hansda, ward member Rooplal Bhumij, technical expert Shankar Kaivart and CRP Dulal Mandal. Women mates were informed about the various provisions of MGNREGA, working methodology of measurement and their responsibilities among other things.
