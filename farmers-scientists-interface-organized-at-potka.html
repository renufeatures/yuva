Yuva organized a Farmer-Scientist Interface at Potka, Jamshedpur

Jamshedpur:  A farmer-scientist interface programme was organized on December 3, 2012 at Potka. The scientists of Krishi Vigyan Kendra, Darisai, listened to the farmers' problems and offered solutions to those problems. Farmers from more than 10 villages of the Potka area took part in the programme very enthusiastically.
Dr. R M Mishra, G Mardi and Bhushan Singh, all scientists from Krishi Vigyan Kendra told the farmers about the different ways that can be used for increasing farm production.

Yuva's farmer-scientist interface at Potka

The local farmers asked many questions which were answered by the scientists.
The scientists also spoke at length about the methods that can easily be adopted by the farmers for increasing their farm produce. They also shared modern techniques of animal husbandry.

Yuva's farmer-scientist interface

All the farmers described the programme as useful and requested Yuva, Jamshedpur to keep organizing such programmes even in future.
