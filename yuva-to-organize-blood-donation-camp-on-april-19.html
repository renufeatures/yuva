Yuva to organize blood donation camp on April 19

By yuvajamshedpur
March 25, 2015
0
Yuva members donating blood

Jamshedpur: The social organization Yuva Jamshedpur would organize a blood donation camp on April 19, 2015 at Yuva Library, Makdampur, Parsudih. The blood donation camp would begin at 9 a.m. and would continue till 3 p.m..

Blood donation camp is an important part of Yuva's annual agenda and the organization has been organizing such camps every year.
A large number of donors take care to attend this camp and donate blood in the greater interest of society.

You are also invited to come to the camp and donate blood or help others donate blood.
