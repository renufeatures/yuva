Free Health Camp in Yuva Library a big hit

By yuvajamshedpur
October 7, 2012
0
Yuva Health Camp in progress

Jamshedpur: A free health was organized here jointly by Catholic Charity and Yuva  at Yuva Library, Madkampur, Jamshedpur. More than 270 patients were treated in that camp free by the doctors.  The patients were also given free medicines on behalf of Mercy Hospital, Baridih.

The doctors who provided their services in the medical camp included a team of doctors from Mercy Hospital, including pediatrician  Dr. Abhishek Kumar; Dr. R K Mohanty, registrar, surgery; Dr. Kanta Kullu, senior medical officer; Dr Zia-ul Haque and Dr. Amit Kumar Singh.  Jesse, administrator,  Mercy Hospital co-ordinated the health camp, while Sister Chritilda, Sister Mauli, Sister Vimla, Sister Elsi, Sister Sushma and Jagat, co-ordinator, Catholic Charity helped in making the health camp a big success.

Yuva Health Camp in progress

Yuva founder member Arbind Tiwary, Mariam Masih of Mahila Vikas Sahyog Samity, Chandmani Sawaiyan, Salomi Barla , Shalini Srivastava, Usha Sabina Deogam,  Manji Topno, Lalita, Dilip, Amit, Anil, Ravi, Ranjan and Sonu of Yuva  also helped in organizing he health camp.

Yuva Health Camp in progress

Yuva has been organizing such health camps for the marginalized sections of society in the Makdampur area of Jamshedpur regularly.  It also organizes blood donation camp regularly.
