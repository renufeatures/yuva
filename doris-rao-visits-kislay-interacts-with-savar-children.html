Doris Rao visits 'Kislay', interacts with Savar Children

By yuvajamshedpur
May 10, 2012
0
Doris Rao interacting with Savar children at Kislay

Jamshedpur: Doris Rao, Consutant, Development Focus visited Kislay, Savar Community School run by YUVA and Asha For Education.

She came here for the organization assessment under the project "Shiksa se parivartan' supported by EDUKANS and Devlopment Focus.

She also visited SHG cluster meeting and praised the work being done by YUVA. She spoke with Yuva members and gave her expert advice on how to make the learning process easier for the Savar children. We thank her for the concern shown by her for the Savar children and her good wishes for making Kislay project a big success.
