Yuva Jamshedpur organizes mason and layout training in Chakdi village, Potka, East Singhbhum

By yuvajamshedpur
February 13, 2013
Layout Training for masons in Chakdi, Potka (Pix: Uttam Acharjee)

Jamshedpur: Yuva, Jamshedpur organized a training programme for masons in Chakdi village in Potka block of East Singhbhum district, Jharkhand. This program was organized for masons under the SCA to TSP project being run with the help of JTDS.

Working masons from eight nearby villages took part in this mason and layout training programme.  Devashish Kamila and Supriyo Bose of Yuva conducted the training programme.

Mason Training in Chakdi village, Potka (Pix: Uttam Acharjee)

The two trainers told the masons in detail about the agro based entrepreneurship buildings proposed to be constructed in all eight villages and also told them about the building layout, map and estimates.

A demo layout was also conducted in the training programme.
