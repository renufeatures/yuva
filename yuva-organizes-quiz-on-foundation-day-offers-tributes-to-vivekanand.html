Yuva organizes quiz on Foundation Day, offers tributes to Vivekanand

By yuvajamshedpur
January 12, 2015
Yuva observes Foundation Day (Pix: Uttam Acharjee)

Jamshedpur: The members of social organization Youth Unity For Voluntary Action (Yuva), Jamshedpur today celebrated its Foundation day at Yuva Library at Makdampur, Parsudih. A quiz was organized on the occasion to mark the day. The team comprising Ranjit, Dilip and Anshu won the quiz, while the team comprising Sumit Sinha and Anit Yadav became the runners up. The quiz was conducted by Md. Naushad.

Foundation Day programme of Yuva was formally inaugurated by the founder members of the organization, Arbind Tiwary and president Varun Kumar.
Addressing the programme Arbind Tiwary said that Yuva has been leading youths in East Singhbhum district for the last 18 years and has been lauded for its role amidst marginalized sections of the society.

He told Yuva members that consistent and continuous effort can help one achieve any target. He advised them not to be disappointed by failures. He said that Yuva's current members should take inspiration from more than 200 members who have succeeded in different competitive examination by dint of their hard work and commitment.

The programme was compered by treasurer Narendra Kumar.
