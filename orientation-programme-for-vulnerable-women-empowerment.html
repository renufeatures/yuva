Orientation Programme for vulnerable women empowerment

By yuvajamshedpur
October 2, 2012
0
Yuva Jamshedpur core team meeting

Jamshedpur: Yuva organized Orientation Programme for empowering vulnerable women at  the coordination office of  Yuva, Jamshedpur. Mr Manas Das ,secretary, an NGO TRCSC was addressed the orientation programme as a resource person. All the members of the core team of YUVA participated in the progrmme enthusiastically. YUVA has initiated special initiatives for empowering vulnerable women with the support of Global Green Grants Fund(GGF) in June month.

Manas Das speaking to Yuva Jamshedpur core team

As a part of this programme,   the core team revisited the project Goal. Manas Das gave the technical  input about creating and maintaining home-cum-nutrition gardens.
