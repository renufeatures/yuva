Yuva to open new school at Savar Nagar in Tangrain, Potka

By yuvajamshedpur
April 3, 2011

Jamshedpur: Thanks to the ardent advocacy by some of Yuva's members, the organization has finally decided to open up a new school for Savar Children in Tangrain, Potka.

Efforts are on to seek help from other organizations and agencies for making the proposed school a reality. The school will cater to the special needs of Savar children who are completely unwilling to join the mainstream education.

All efforts from the government to pull Savar children into the mainstream of education have come to a naught, thanks to the traditional coyness of Savar community.

Yuva has been planning a new initiative for Savar children for quite some time. Discussions were initiated even in the past for doing something concrete in this direction.

The good news is that there is some glimmer of hope as plans are taking shape and would soon be finalized.
