Workshop on MNREGA in Dhirol village, Potka

By yuvajamshedpur
January 1, 2015
MNREGA seminar in Dhirol, Potka by Yuva (Pix: Uttam Acharjee)

Jamshedpur: The premiere social youth organization Youth Unity For Voluntary Action (Yuva, Jamshedpur) organized a panchayat-level workshop on MNREGA in Dhirol village of Potka block in East Singhbhum.
Yuva members Abhishek Sardar, Chandmani Sawaiyan and Usha Deogam addressed this seminar and apprised all the villagers present about the process of preparing Village development Plan under IPPE.

They said that this process ensures participation of people of weaker and poor sections of the society in the plan preparation.

Those who were present in the workshop included Nimai Chandra Gope, member, panchayat samity, Dhirol Gram Panchayat, Raghunath Murmu, gram pradhan, Dhirol, Man Singh Soren, Gram Pradhan of Virvad Tola, Subhas Sardar, gram pradhan of Roldih, Heeralal Barik of Dhirol, Abhirodh Gope, ward member, Bango and Rajit Lagat of Dhirol.
