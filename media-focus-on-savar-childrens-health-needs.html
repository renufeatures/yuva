Media focus on Savar children's health needs

By yuvajamshedpur
May 25, 2011
0

Jamshedpur: Dainik Jagran, a leading newspaper of Jamshedpur, has highlighted the heath needs of Savar children of Tangrain, Potka. In a 4-column report the newspaper has mentioned the health needs of Savar children and has also talked of the initiative of Yuva and Asha For Education to open a Savar Community School in the area to help Savar children in joining the mainstream of education.

It is hoped that the media coverage would highlight the problems of Savar children and the government would take note of it and do something special for the Savar children and the entire Savar Community keeping in mind their 'very special' cultural and community context and setting.
