Yuva Jamshedpur organizes blood donation camp on its foundation day

Yuva Jamshedpur organized a blood donation camp at Yuva Library in Makdampur, Parsudih on the occasion of its foundation day, which is also birth anniversary of Swami Vivekanand.

Its young member Md Wahid set a record by donating blood on the very next day of having completed 18 years of age.

Blood donation camp at Yuva Jamshedpur Libary in Makdampur. (Pix: Uttam Acharjee)

The newly elected district councilor Rakhi Guha, mukhiya of Kalimati Mr. Umesh, Ward member Chanchal Shrivastava and Asgari Begam were also felicitated on the occasion.

The guest of honour of the programme were well-known social activist Mrs Anjali Bose and Harvansh Lal Sriastava.

Yuva organizes Planning-2015 Workshop in Potka

Planning Campaign 2015 under process in Potka. (Pix: Uttam Acharjee).

Jamshedpur: The social organization Youth Unity For Voluntary Action (YUVA) organized a one-day "Yojana Banao Abhiyan ( Planning 2015 Campaign)" at the Meeting Hall at Potka Block Headquarters Premises.

The workshop was organized under MGNREGA CFT Convergence Project.  In all 23 Rojgar Sevaks and 12 CFT employees participated in this workshop. The programme was inaugurated by the Block Program Officer Mr. Santosh Kumar.

Yuva saves four minor girls from child marriage

Bali Sabar(Pix: Uttam Acharjee)

Jamshedpur: Yuva Jamshedpur, the social organization, has saved four minor Sabar girls from the curse of child marriage in the month of March.

A few days back two minor Sabar girls of Dhengam village in Potka block suddenly vanished from the village. It may be mentioned that the social organization Yuva has been running a learning centre for Sabar children with the help of CWS and ASW in Dhengam village.
