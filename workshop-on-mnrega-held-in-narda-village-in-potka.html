Workshop on MNREGA held in Narda village in Potka

By yuvajamshedpur
January 1, 2015
0
Jamshedpur: The premiere social youth organization Youth Unity For Voluntary Action (Yuva, Jamshedpur) organized a panchayat-level workshop on MNREGA in Narda village of Potka block in East Singhbhum.

Addressing the workshop Barnali Chakraborty, secretary, Yuva said that Yuva Jamshedpur has been entrusted the responsibility of implementing the CFT Project on behalf of Jharkhand State Livelihood Promotion Society (JSLPS) with a view to making MNREGA more effective in the entire Potka Block. Yuva, Jamshedpur is preparing village development plans under IPPE in 50 villages spread over six panchayats of Potka. 

Expressing their views in the workshop the villagers said that several poor villagers and daily wagers have not been issued job cards yet. Only 5% people living in the NREGA panchayat have been issued job cards.

The workshop was attended by people's representatives and Gram Pradhan.
