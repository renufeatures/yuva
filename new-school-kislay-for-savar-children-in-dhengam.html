New School 'Kislay' for Savar children in Dhengam

By yuvajamshedpur
November 17, 2011
0

The school was formally inaugurated on November 15, the 12th anniversary of Jharkhand.

The first such school "Kislay" has already been running in Sabar Nagar, Tangrain for the last six months.

Yuva secretary, Barnali said that Yuva remains committed to spread education among Savar children and this new unit in Dhengam would provide an opportunity to Savar chidren of that area to join a school and be ready to join the mainstream of education.
