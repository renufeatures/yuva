Workshop on quality education, RTE Act and mainstreaming of Sabar Children held

By yuvajamshedpur
October 7, 2013
0

Jamshedpur: YUVA, Jamshedpur organised a one-day workshop on quality education, RTE act and mainstreaming of Sabar children at Dhengam village of Potka.  The workshop was organized with the help of Centre For World Solidarity( CWS) and ASW..

Workshop on Quality Education (Pix: Uttam Acharjee)

Education is the only key to change the fate of future generations of the deprived sections fo the society, especially the tribals who have not received the benefit of development for a long time due to socio-economic reasons.
Even among tribals, Sabars are perhaps the most vulnerable community and they need special attention from the society.

So spoke Arbind Tiwary, the founder member of YUVA as a resource person in the workshop.

He said that only education will and can change the fate of sabar children.

He also narrated the pros and cons of RTE Act.

Workshop on Quality Education (Pix: Uttam Acharjee)

Mr. Mantu Goswami, Teacher of Dhengam middle school, Mr Miru Sardar, Mukhiya(Headman of Village Dhengam), Panchayat samity member Sonamuni Murmu, Sajan murmu, ward member Chandmani sardar, Cordinater Arup kumar mandal,Mejati sardar,Hambir Murmu also expressed their ideas in he workshop.

Abhishek Sardar conducted the workshop.
